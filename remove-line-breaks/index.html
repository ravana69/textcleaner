Remove Line Breaks

Remove line breaks is an online tool to remove line breaks or replace line breaks with specific character and it has few additional features that makes your task even more easier.

Text Cleaner has handy text tools and Remove line breaks is one of those popular tool. It has very popular features including remove line breaks or replace line break with a character. Use this tool by entering your text or uploading file and selecting appropriate available options to remove line breaks.

How to Remove Line Breaks Online

To use our Remove line breaks online tool follow these steps:

  • Step 1: Enter your text or upload a word file.
  • Step 2: Then select remove one or replace with or remove all line breaks.
  • Step 3: Now, click on Remove button. Use clear option to start again.
  • Step 4: Download or Save your output as word document.

Features of Remove Line Breaks

  • Use this Remove line breaks online tool to remove all existing line breaks.
  • You can also replace line breaks with a different character.
  • Upload your text file or create one by selecting file drop down menu.
  • Then edit it with remove line breaks or replace with a character options and save.

Remove One

Select “Replace One” option and then click on “clear” button to remove line breaks. This is useful if you have a double line spaced text document that you want to convert into single-spaced.

Example

The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog

The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog

The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog

Result

The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog
The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog
The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog

Replace With

Select “Replace With” option, enter a character of your choice and click on “clear” button to replace all existing line breaks.

Example: Replace line breaks with (. ) period followed by space.

input

The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog
The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog
The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog

Result

The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog. The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog. The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog

Remove All

Select “Remove All” option and click on “clear” button to delete all existing line breaks.
