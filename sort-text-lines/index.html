How to Sort Text online?

Follow below steps to sort text online:

  • Step 1: Enter text lines or list to sort things.
  • Step 2: Select sorting options such as alphabetical, reverse, shuffle, character length or natural.
  • Step 3: Optionally enable case sensitive option for exact match.
  • Step 4: Finally click on sort button to get sorted list based on selected option.

That’s it!
